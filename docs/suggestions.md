# RASCAL | Feature Suggestions

List of brainstormed features to make this anime tracker even better for Master! (Nya~)

## 🚀 High Priority
- **MAL / AniList Integration**: Direct import/export with MyAnimeList and AniList. No more manual tracking, meow!
- **Global Search**: Search TMDb directly from the search bar to find and add new anime instantly.
- **Enhanced Data Sync**: Cloud backup (Google Drive/Dropbox) for the `data.json` file so Master never loses his list.

## 🎨 UI/UX Enhancements
- **Visual Progress Bars**: A sleek bar on each card showing exactly how far Master has progressed through a series.
- **Anime Airing Calendar**: A dedicated tab to see which seasonal anime are airing today/this week.
- **Custom Themes**: Let Master pick his own accent colors. I'll make sure they look pretty... not that I care!
- **Hover Previews**: Show a brief synopsis or trailer when hovering over a card (from TMDb).

## ⚙️ Logic & Features
- **Auto-Next Episode**: Automatically update the internal tracker and move to the next episode after clicking the play button.
- **"Pick for Me" (Randomizer)**: Can't decide what to watch? Let the app pick a random title from the "Te Bekijken" list.
- **Advanced Filtering**: Filter by genres (Action, Romance, etc.), release year, or rating.
- **Watch History**: A detailed log of when each episode was watched.

## 📱 Mobile Optimization
- **PWA Support**: Make the tracker installable as an app on Master's phone for tracking on the go!
- **Swipe Actions**: Swipe cards left/right to change status (Bekeken/Bezig).
